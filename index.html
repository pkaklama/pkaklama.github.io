Summary:
I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Maxwell Institute for Mathematical Sciences, University of Edinburgh. My research focusses on theory and applications of dynamical systems, in particular on the study of systems that feature multiple timescales, using geometric singular perturbation theory.

I completed my PhD in Applied & Computational Mathematics at the Maxwell Institute Gaduate School (MIGS), University of Edinburgh. I followed the Industry Stream offered by MIGS, and I was president of the Edinburgh SIAM-IMA Student Chapter during my final year.
